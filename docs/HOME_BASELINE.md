# Home Prologue Baseline

StatForge v1.2 offers a three-session Home Prologue for users who want to unlock character creation before reaching the gym.

## Equipment

- Clear floor space
- A sturdy chair placed against a wall
- A clear wall
- A timer on the phone
- Support nearby for balance when needed

No wearable, heart-rate monitor, step tracker, or third-party fitness app is required.

## What it measures

1. **Push, core, and balance:** 2-minute assessment push-ups, clean forearm-plank time, single-leg balance, and dead-bug control.
2. **Legs and work capacity:** 30-second chair stands, wall-sit time, a 2-minute march count, calf raises, and bird dogs.
3. **Back-to-back trial:** push-ups, the required 2-minute rest, plank, the required 5-minute rest, and a repeatable 2-minute march calibration in place of the rower.

The march result is used only as a personal starting point. StatForge does not compare it to a medical or population norm.

## Character creation and rowing

Completing all three Home Prologue sessions unlocks level-1 character creation. The app then shows an optional 30-minute **2K Row Calibration** for the next time the user reaches a Concept2 rower. The row session refines rowing targets but does not delay character creation.

## Safety design

The route uses controlled bodyweight tasks and ordinary movement patterns, with warm-ups, rest periods, technique limits, and a pre-workout pain check. Stop the session for sharp or unusual pain, chest discomfort, faintness, severe shortness of breath, or other concerning symptoms.
